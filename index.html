📊 七個睡眠向度分析 | 7 Sleep Component Scores

各向度 0–3 分（0 = 無問題，3 = 嚴重問題）；總分 0–21 分，>5 分表示睡眠品質差
Each component 0–3 (0=no difficulty, 3=severe difficulty); total >5 = poor sleep quality

📈 睡眠向度雷達圖 | Sleep Component Radar

🩺 重要提醒 | Important Reminder

本量表（PSQI）為標準化睡眠品質篩查工具，不能取代臨床診斷。
若總分 > 5 分，建議諮詢睡眠醫學科、身心科或神經科醫師進行進一步評估。

The PSQI is a standardized screening instrument and does not constitute a clinical diagnosis.
A total score >5 suggests poor sleep quality; please consult a physician for formal evaluation.

Buysse DJ et al. (1989). The Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index. Psychiatry Research, 28(2), 193–213.
